Summary

The form of error control based on sliding-window flow control that is most commonly used is called go-back-N ARQ. In this method, a station may send a series of frames sequentially numbered modulo some maximum value. The number of unacknowledged frames outstanding is determined by window size, using the sliding-window flow control technique.
